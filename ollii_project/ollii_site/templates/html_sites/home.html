OLLII

Online Library for Learning Implementation and Innovation (OLLII) is a project with a focus on developing re-usable knowledge resources, search and data mining tools, and interactive learning for capacity building and training in the area of maternity health and child development. It draws on cutting-edge domain research and computing technologies for developing resources and it utilizes implementation science as both a target and a foundational domain to offer informatics services. The project team includes experts in public health, medicine, and clinical informatics, based in the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
